Beginners

Training plan for beginners

The workout for beginners is based on basic exercise. They are designed to prepare the body for exercise with a higher load with physical development. The plan is adapted both for people starting from scratch and for those who already do some training on their own. Below you will find a plan prepared by our qualified trainers, which will allow you to start working on your physical condition in an easy and pleasant way.

Monday Name [series no; reps; tempo; break] description
1 Squads 3|10|2010|120s

1) Maintaining the correct starting position and the natural curvature of the spine, perform a smooth and slow squat to a position of about a right angle, along with a slow inhalation of air. 2) Exhaling, stand up to the starting position. Do not overextend the knees. 3) Perform the designated number of repetitions.
2 Launges 3|12|1010|180s

Looking ahead we take a step forward with one of our feet. Landing gently on your heel, place your whole foot forward. When the thigh of the outstretched leg is horizontal to the ground, push off from it by straightening the knee and return to the starting position. Exhale at the end of the movement.
3 Push-ups 3|15|2020|120s

A push-up (pronounced: forward bending and straightening of the arms) is a popular name for a physical exercise performed in a horizontal position, facing the ground, which involves raising and lowering the torso.
